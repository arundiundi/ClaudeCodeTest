The Lifetime and $12.00 annual dues are used to defray the administrative costs associated with the organizational requirements to maintain and periodically distribute a roster and periodic hard copy newsletter, maintain a current web page on the internet, and purchase office supplies like paper, envelopes, stamps and the like.


Reunions conducted every year are planned to be self-funded. Excess earnings are distributed to approved tax-exempt veterans organizations.
